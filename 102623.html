adding outside games onto the school ipads

I wanted to get outside apps onto the school ipad (which has a shitton of useless restrictions, part of why MNblocker was started). Basically sideloading and I wanted to get flappybird on the home screen. After a bit of research I found you could download a zip file for flappy bird on the internet archive.

Once downloaded, makes sure its not in the “On My iPad” section in Garageband. (on the school ipads, the only way to view and edit files is on garageband. annoying but it works ig) It has to be on onedrive. This is really only possible if you have a phone or computer or your own personal device. On your personal device install onedrive and extract the zip file you downloaded from the archive. Then you can move it into On My iPad (or else it wont display in garageband). Then you can rename the unzipped file to .app or something else I don’t now yet. This is all very new. New stuff to be done in the coming weeks, hopefully itll be figured out soon.
